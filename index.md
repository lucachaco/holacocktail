## Margarita Frozen
- 2 oz Tequila
- 1 oz Triple Sec
- 6 oz Sour Mix
- 1 drop of green colorant (optional)

### Glass
- Schooner glass 20 oz full of ice.

### Decoration
- Salt rim
- Lemon slice

### Technique
Blend everything with the ice from the glass.
